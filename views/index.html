About RUDRAM YOG
The RUDRAM YOG was started by Mrs. Kanchan Patil. Mrs Kanchan Patil did her Diploma and TTC from Mumbai university and became an ISO certified yoga teacher. Practicing yoga for over an years, she is a senior yoga teacher and gained a lot of experience as a yoga teacher by teaching at yogdhenu. Kanchan Patil is also a Children's Yoga teacher, with further qualifications in restorative, pregnancy, postnatal & hot yoga.
