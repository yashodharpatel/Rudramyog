About RUDRAMYOG

The RUDRAM YOG was started by Mrs. Kanchan Patil. Mrs. Kanchan Patil did her Diploma and TTC from Mumbai university and became an ISO certified yoga teacher. Practicing yoga for over years, she is a senior yoga teacher and gained a lot of experience as a yoga teacher by teaching at yogdhenu. Kanchan Patil is also a Children's Yoga teacher, with further qualifications in restorative, pregnancy, postnatal & hot yoga.


Well, despite practicing yoga for over years and taking classes offline as well as online during pandemic with plots of positive testimonials from our students. Also seen a lot of awareness and improvement in health-related problems in some clients. Through Rudramyog we want to connect with more people and make them feel healthy and happy as yoga heals everything if practiced perfectly with proper guidance. We aim to spread awareness about yoga in our society, We want everyone to be healthy and happy in society and want every kid to be a yogi in the future.
